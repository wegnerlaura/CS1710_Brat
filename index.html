Introduction

Charli XCX, born Charlotte Aitchison, is a revolutionary force in modern pop music, blending experimental production with infectious hooks to redefine the genre. From her early days uploading music to MySpace and performing at underground raves, to global fame with hits like Boom Clap and I Love It, Charli has always pushed boundaries. Her groundbreaking work in hyperpop, notably with albums like Pop 2 and Charli, showcases collaborations with avant-garde producers and artists. Now, with the Brat era, she embraces raw energy and bold themes, solidifying her legacy as a fearless artist uniting mainstream success with experimental edge.
